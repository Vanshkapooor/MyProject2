Deganwy, UK

Deganwy is a small town in Conwy County Borough in Wales with a population of 3,936 (2011). It lies in the Creuddyn Peninsula alongside Llandudno (to the north) and Rhos-on-Sea (to its east). The built-up area of Deganwy Junction had a population of 10,658.

Western Desert, Egypt

The Western Desert of Egypt is an area of the Sahara that lies west of the river Nile, up to Libyan border, and from the Mediterranean sea to the border with Sudan. It is named in contrast to the Eastern Desert which extends east from the Nile to Red Sea.

Manali, India

Manali is a resort and tourist town in the mountains of the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh near the Kullu Valley in the Beas River Valley. It is located in the Kullu district, about 270 km north of the state capital, Shimla. Manali is named after the Sanatan Hindu lawgiver Manu.
